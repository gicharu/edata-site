RENTAL CONTRACT REGISTRATION

 


The development of standardized rental agreements and establishing centralized registries for such agreements facilitates setting of rental costs for single homes, multiple residential properties and commercial buildings and simplifies the of municipal authorities, which finance the delivery of public services in part from taxation of rental incomes. The Info.Africa Platform shall, using its E-Transaction and E-Trust Tools, provide a centralized and searchable rental contract registration portal, which real estate professionals, investors, lenders and tax authorities can access for their purposes.
